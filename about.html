Our Annual Calendar of Events

Our academic year is packed with a diverse range of events that cater to all interests. Here’s a glimpse of our key annual happenings:

Technical Events

  • Robotics Championship

    February

    An electrifying competition where teams design, build, and battle their own robots.

  • TechFest & Hackathon

    September

    A 48-hour coding marathon combined with a week of tech talks and workshops.

Cultural Events

  • Annual Music Night

    October

    An evening featuring performances from student bands and renowned local artists.

  • Cultural Day Gala

    December

    A celebration of diversity with traditional performances, food, and a dance competition.

Sports & Community Events

  • Inter-College Sports Meet

    January

    A week of thrilling athletic competition for the championship trophy.

  • Blood Donation Drive

    March

    A campus-wide initiative to give back to the community and save lives.

  • Alumni Meet

    May

    Graduates reconnect, network, and share experiences with current students.
